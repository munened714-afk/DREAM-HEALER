Who We Are

Driven by impact, structured for transformation.

Dream Healer International is a community-centered organization committed to transforming lives through mentorship, education, leadership development, financial empowerment, and mental wellness. We work with schools, churches, institutions, and communities to equip individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to make informed decisions and build a purpose-driven future. We believe that every individual has unique potential. Through practical training, guidance, and empowerment programs, we inspire people to overcome challenges, discover opportunities, and become positive contributors to their families, communities, and society..

🎯

Our Mission

To empower high school students, junior secondary learners, tertiary students, and young people through mentorship, career guidance, leadership development, financial literacy, mental wellness, and life-skills education, enabling them to make informed decisions, realize their full potential, and become responsible leaders in their communities.

👁️

Our Vision

To raise a generation of informed, empowered, and purpose-driven learners and young leaders who positively transform their communities and the world.

Glory Kathambi

Founder

Glory Kathambi Kithetu is the Founder and Executive Director of Dream Healer International. She is a passionate mentor, educator, and community leader dedicated to empowering learners and young people to discover their purpose, build confidence, and unlock their full potential. Driven by a deep commitment to transforming lives, she has worked with schools, churches, institutions, and communities, delivering impactful mentorship programs in career guidance, leadership development, financial literacy, mental wellness, and personal growth. Glory believes that every learner deserves guidance, encouragement, and opportunities to succeed. Through Dream Healer International, she continues to inspire hope, nurture future leaders, and create lasting positive change in communities.
